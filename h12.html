Benefits


The benefits you’re entitled to as a member of Flying Returns range from free air tickets to discounts and special offers from our Alliances. So, every time you fly with us or utilize the services of our alliance partners, you gain valuable mileage points that could entitle you to one or more of these benefits.

 

 

 

Accrual of Mileage Points

Each time you fly on Bharat Airlines, Indian Airlines, Alliance Air, Lufthansa or any of our Code-Share partners, you can accrue mileage points.

You can accrue Mileage Points for travel on any sector in any class on all tickets issued on promotional fares, except:

- Tickets issued against Transportation Service Contract.

- ID/AD Tickets.

- Free/Non-Revenue /Award Tickets.

- Tickets not indicating published fares.

Our Alliance Partnerships with Leading Travel Related Organisations give you many more opportunities.

Procedure for accrual

- Quote your Flying Returns Membership number while making your 
  reservations.

- Ensure that your membership number appears on the ticket.

IMPORTANT

Present your membership card to the Check-in Staff. Retain your Boarding passes till you receive the Mileage Points credit. These are reflected in the Statement forwarded to you once every two months.

Members of The Maharajah Club and the Leading Edge Club must quote their TMC/LEC membership numbers for accrual of mileage points. ONLY IF mileage points are not credited to your account, then forward your Boarding Passes and Ticket Jackets to the Member Service Centre for credit of mileage points.
