Refunds

1.       Is the Bharat Airlines website secure enough for passengers to provide their credit card information?
The Bharat Airlines website is completely secure and is endorsed by Verisign. All the confidential payment details of our passengers are encrypted and stored so as to ensure maximum security.



2.       Is there a specific browser that has to be used to book Bharat Airlines tickets?
The Bharat Airlines website can only be browsed by Internet Explorer Version 5.5 and above. Other browsers like Mozilla, Netscape etc do not support the website and Bharat Airlines tickets cannot be booked through them.

 

3.       What happens if while booking my card gets debited but the PNR is not generated?
At times while booking tickets due to poor internet connectivity or slow speed, the payment goes through but the transaction does not complete and the PNR is not generated. However, a Reference Number will be generated which will help you track the booking. It will display the PNR if it has been generated and give a message if it has not been generated.


4.       What is the importance of noting the Reference Number which is generated while booking tickets?
The reference number helps in tracking whether a transaction has been successful or not and whether the ticket has been confirmed or not. A unique reference number is generated every time a transaction is taking place on the website. Please note that reference number without fail and incase the transaction does not go through (PNR does not get generated) or an error message is displayed or you are unsure please use this number to track your transaction. This also helps in avoiding multiple PNR generation and excess debits.

 

5.       Often while trying to cancel tickets, the website gives an error and the process does not get completed. What do I do in such cases?
For safety issues the security settings of all web browsers is set to high which does not allow the ticket to be cancelled. Please change your browser settings to low and then try canceling the ticket.

 

6.        

How do I collect my refund and when?
The refund has to be collected from the point of purchase of the ticket. For e.g. if the ticket has been purchased from ABC Travel Agency, the refund will also have to be collected from ABC Travel Agency itself. For online bookings, the refund rolls back to the credit card used for booking the ticket. All refunds take at least 14 working days to be processed.




 

 

7.       Why does it take 14 days for refunds to be processed?
Most of the refunds are automatic and roll back with 7 days of the refund request. However, at times there are cases which require manual intervention and the refund has to be processed manually which takes times. Also very often, there are issues like payment gateway breakdowns, bank holidays, credit cards being blocked, expired credit cards etc which delay refunds even up to 30 days.
