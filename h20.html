Booking Queries

 

  1. How do I make the payment?
    For bookings made online and through the call centre the payment has to be remitted by credit card. At other points of sale, payment can be made either via cash or by credit card.

 

  1. Is the Bharat Airlines website secure enough for passengers to provide their credit card information?
    The Bharat Airlines website is completely secure and is endorsed by Verisign. All the confidential payment details of our passengers are encrypted and stored so as to ensure maximum security.  

 

  1. Is there a specific browser that has to be used to book Bharat Airlines tickets?
    Bharat Airlines website can only be browsed by Internet Explorer Version 5.5 and above. Other browsers like Mozilla, Netscape etc do not support the website and Bharat Airlines tickets cannot be booked through them.

 

  1. What is the mandatory information that should be provided while booking a ticket?
    It is absolutely essential to provide the passenger’s correct mobile number on the ticket. Bharat Airlines sends SMS alerts to mobile numbers of passengers entered while booking, to inform of any changes in schedule. If your ticket is being booked by a travel agent, please ensure that your mobile number is entered instead of the agents. It is also essential to provide an alternate land line number, your email address and valid contact address while booking.

Re-scheduling and cancellation 

  1. How many hours prior to departure am I allowed to re-schedule my journey?
    Rescheduling of tickets is only possible 04 hours prior to flight departure. Nochange is possible within 04 hours of flight departure. Within 4 hours before departure, it is only possible to cancel the reservation.

 

  1. What is the re-scheduling fee?
    The rescheduling fee is Rs. 200/- per passenger per journey. The difference in fare is also charged.

 

  1. How do I re-schedule my ticket?
    Tickets can be re-scheduled at any point of sale.

 

  1. Am I entitled to any refund incase I cancel my booking? If yes, what?
    - Passengers who cancel their tickets are entitled to a refund, however a cancellation fee is charged. The same is mentioned below.

    For Tickets purchased for travel dates upto 29th October 2006

    - 10% of the basic fare + transaction fee is charged if the cancellation is made more than 72 hours from the scheduled departure time.

    - 20% of the basic fare + transaction fee is charged if the cancellation is made less than 72 hours but greater than 48 hours from the scheduled departure time.

    - 30% of the basic fare + transaction fee is charged if the cancellation is made less than 48 hours but greater than 24 hours from the scheduled departure time.

    - 50% of the basic fare + transaction fee is charged if the cancellation is made less than 24 hours but greater than 1 hour from the scheduled departure time.

    For tickets purchased for travel dates 30th October 2006 onwards

    - 15% of the basic fare + transaction fee if the cancellation is made more than 72 hours from the scheduled departure time.

    - 25% of the basic fare + transaction fee if the cancellation is made less than 72 hours but greater than 48 hours from the scheduled departure time.

    - 35% of the basic fare + transaction fee if the cancellation is made less than 48 hours but greater than 24 hours from the scheduled departure time.

    - 55% of the basic fare + transaction fee if the cancellation is made less than 24 hours but greater than 1 hour from the scheduled departure time.

    - Only taxes are refunded in case the passenger is a No-Show or cancels within 1 hour from the scheduled departure time. No refund is given to ‘No Show’ passengers who do not report either at the check-in counter or the boarding gate on time.

 

  1. How do I cancel my ticket?
    Tickets can be cancelled at any point of sale.

 

  1. Often while trying to cancel tickets, the website gives an error and the process does not get completed. What do I do in such cases?
    For safety issues web browsers by default set the security settings to high which does not allow the ticket to be cancelled. Please change your browser settings to low and then try canceling the ticket

 

  1. How do I collect my refund and when?
    The refund has to be collected from the point of purchase of the ticket. For e.g. if the ticket has been purchased from ABC Travel Agency, the refund will also have to be collected from ABC Travel Agency itself. For online bookings, the refund rolls back to the credit card used for booking the ticket. All refunds take at least 14 working days to be processed.

 


Check-In  

  1.  When do I check-in?
    Bharat Airlines check-in counters open 2 hours prior to scheduled flight departure and close strictly 30 minutes prior to flight departure. Passengers who do not comply with the check-in deadline and report late are deemed as ‘no-show’ passengers and are denied boarding without refund.

 

  1. Is there a deadline for the security check after check-in? 
    All passengers have to report at the security hold and boarding gate area at least 20 minutes prior to flight departure. Passengers who do not report in time are deemed as ‘gate no-shows’ and are denied boarding without refund.

 

  1. Do large groups of passengers have a different check-in deadline? 
    Groups larger than 10 passengers have to report at the check-in counter at least 1.5 hours prior to departure.

 

  1. Does Bharat Airlines allow through check-in?
    Bharat Airlines does not allow through check-in either for passengers or for baggage.  

 

  1. Incase I have an Bharat Airlines connecting flight on the same day, what should be the time gap between the arrival and departure? 
    Incase a passenger has an Bharat Airlines connecting or return flight on the same day, there has to be a minimum gap of 2 hours between the arrival of the onward flight and departure of the return or connecting Bharat Airlines flight.

 

Changes in flight schedule (delays, advancements and cancellations)

  1. How does Bharat Airlines inform passengers about changes in schedule?
    Bharat Airlines schedules are subject to change without notice. Bharat Airlines notifies all passengers of any scheduled change via SMS which is sent to the mobile numbers they provide at the time of booking. There are no phone calls made to inform passengers. Every passenger is requested to kindly confirm, either with the call centre at (your city code) - 39008888 or the travel agent to confirm the status of the flight at least 3 hrs prior to the scheduled departure of the flight.

 

  1. What happens incase an Bharat Airlines flight gets delayed or cancelled?
    If in case of unavoidable reasons Bharat Airlines cancels its flight or delays it beyond two hours, as per the present policy, the passengers are either entitled to a full refund or for a free re-scheduling to a later date subject to availability. Incase you cancel your booking; the refund will have to be collected at the point of purchase of your ticket.  We wish to add, incase of flight delay beyond two hours, the passengers have to cancel or re-schedule the ticket before the departure of the flight else the ticket becomes no-show and is not entitled for any refund or re-scheduling.

 

 

  1. What happens in case of weather related flight disruptions?
    For certain months in the year especially Mid-December and Mid-January, due to poor visibility, dense fog and bad weather in North India some flights may get cancelled, re-scheduled, indefinitely delayed or diverted to other airfields. These circumstances are completely beyond the control of the airline and are governed by relevant regulatory authorities.  In such cases Bharat Airlines’s responsibility is restricted to transporting the passenger only to the destination booked. The airline is not liable to provide alternate transport, accommodation or refreshments at any point in time and the passengers have to make their own arrangements.

 

  1. What happens in case I have a connecting flight after an Bharat Airlines flight and the Bharat Airlines flight gets delayed?
    Bharat Airlines is strictly a 'point to point' airline and does not take any obligation or responsibility for transfer of passengers or their baggage to other flights, whether operated by Bharat Airlines or another carrier. Bharat Airlines does not provide for accommodation, refreshments nor does it provide or arrange for alternate mode of travel. 


    Special services (wheelchair and unaccompanied minors)
  2. What special services does Bharat Airlines provide to its passengers?
    Bharat Airlines offers special assistance for passengers who require wheelchair assistance and to unaccompanied minors. Wheel chair assistance is provided at a charge of Rs 200/- (w.e.f. from 1st June 2006) per journey per passenger and unaccompanied minor assistance at a charge of Rs 500/- per journey per passenger.

 

  1. Why are these services charged for?
    Any service which involves a human element does have a service charge involved and thereby we have introduced a system wherein we charge for special services. Bharat Airlines being a no-frills airline has to charge for these services in order to maintain its low fares.

 

  1. How can I avail of these services?
    These special services have to be mentioned at the time of booking. The payment has to be made at the airport on the day of the journey.

 

  1. What is the mode of payment for these services at the airport?
    Payment at the airport for special services has to be made in cash. Credit and debit cards are not accepted
