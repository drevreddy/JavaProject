Frequent Flyers


What is the criteria to become a member of Flying Returns?
Individuals aged 12 years and above can enrol as members. Flying Returns is at present operative in India, the Gulf, UK, USA and Canada and is open to the residents of the respective countries.

Where can I get Flying Returns application forms?
Download the Flying Returns application forms or from your nearest Bharat Airlines or Indian Airlines office or from the Member Service Centres at Mumbai, Delhi, Calcuta, Chennai and Bangalore.

Can I pool my mileage points with my spouse and family?
Your mileage points can be pooled in with your spouse for the purpose of redeeming free tickets. For this, your spouse must be an independent member of the Programme.
There is no provision for pooling mileage points with other family members.

Do all tickets on Bharat Airlines qualify for mileage?
All tickets purchased in any currency and in any country for travel on Bharat Airlines, Indian Airlines, Alliance Air and Air France and our code-share flights issued against IATA published or normal fares, qualify for accrual of mileage points.
However, free/non-revenue/award tickets issued against Transportation Service Contract, Travel Industry related discounts and specified promotional fares, do not qualify. Tickets on joint-venture flights of Bharat Airlines and Indian Airlines with any other airline also do not qualify for accrual.

How do I redeem my mileage points?
You can redeem any time after you have accrued 20,000 mileage points.
All you need to do is

  • Refer to the mileage points table forwarded to you on enrolment
  • Ensure you have adequate mileage for the free ticket of your choice, and
  • Write to the Member Service Centre in Mumbai in the prescribed format, at least seven days prior to the date of travel.
