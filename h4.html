Lounges

Bharat Airlines has upgraded its lounges at various international airports served by the airline. Bharat Airlines has its own exclusive lounges at Mumbai, Delhi, London, Hong Kong where the First and Executive Class passengers can enjoy our hospitality, relax over drinks and snacks, before boarding their flight. A new lounge will also shortly be commissioned at Chennai. Elsewhere, Bharat Airlines has tie-ups with other international airlines or local Airports Authorities for lounge facilities.


   Mumbai
   There are three lounges at Mumbai's Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport.First and Executive Class passengers can surf the net or     send e-mail in the comfort of our business centre located in the Maharajah     Lounge while awaiting the departure of their flight. The    lounge for transitpassengers is equipped with shower facilities, specially designed slumberettes, a gymnasium and a business centre. There is also a lounge for unaccompanied minors.

Delhi

First and Executive Class passengers can, while awaiting departure of their flight, avail of an Ayurvedic massage in addition to other facilities, such as business centre, library and a music centre in the Maharajah lounge at Delhi's Indira Gandhi International Airport which reflects a world class ambience. Italian marble on the floor and counters, warm, light colour wood on columns, attractive false ceiling and colonade at the rear lobby enhance the appearance of the lounge. While the lounge appears spacious, low dividers provide adequate privacy where required.
