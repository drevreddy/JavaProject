First Time Flyers


When do I have to check-in for a flight?
The check-in counters are open 4 hours prior to departure of the flight. Under normal circumstances, you need to check in at least 45 minutes to an hour before scheduled time of departure of the flight. (subject to change).

What is an Embarkation Form ?
It’s a form asking for your travel particulars that you have to fill up prior to your departure

Can I change money at the airport just prior to departure?
Yes, money exchange counters are available at most airports.

Is the same rule applicable if I break journey?
If you break your journey, you are required to necessarily reconfirm your ticket 72 hrs. prior to departure if you are travelling to any of the destinations in the Middle East.

Is vaccination against disease a mandatory health requirement for travel?
Vaccination against yellow fever is the only vaccination specifically required in normal travel.

Do I have to get vaccinated against Yellow Fever for any sector of International travel?
No; This, as a health regulation, is applicable only under certain specific conditions and more so, specific to certain countries- specifically the infected areas in Africa and South America.

Where can I get the particular details with regards to this?
Your travel agent will inform you regarding current heath regulations of the country of your proposed travel.

What is the validity of a yellow fever vaccination?
The validity is 10 years commencing 10 days after vaccination. However, in case of re-vaccination within the 10 year period, the validity is 10 years beginning from the date of re-vaccination.
