Inflight Merchandise

  1. Does Bharat Airlines sell products on board? Can you please elaborate on that?
    Bharat Airlines sells catalogued products on board supplied by AVA Merchandising. A varied range of personal, office and house-hold products are available in-flight. These are World class brands available at almost 50% of the market prices.

    However, Bharat Airlines is in no way responsible for the delivery, payment, or condition of the products purchased in-flight.

 

  1. How does one make the payment in-flight and when do we get the product?
    Payment can be made either by cash or credit card on the flight. The product is then shipped to the address given by the passenger within 21 working days time.
